Digital minimalism

Why so boringgg?

This website is made with digital minimalism in mind — not just because it's sleek(Or that i'm ignoring the fact that i don't really know CSS), but because less truly is more.

With all the noise online, it's easy to forget what actually matters.

Social media has morphed into a circus, full of bright lights and noise, offering illusions of connection but really just empty distractions.

Instead of stuffing this site with flashy widgets, I kept it simple.

No endless feeds or addictive notifications here. It's a quiet place that respects your attention and maybe... even your sanity.

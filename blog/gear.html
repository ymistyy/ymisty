Thinkpad T490

I use a ThinkPad T490 with 1TB SSD and 16GB RAM, powered by an Intel i5 8th Gen processor.

It’s a reliable laptop for everything I do: coding, browsing, and running multiple apps at the same time.

The SSD is large enough for what i need, and the RAM is enough for heavy tasks.

The T490 might be a bit older, but it still gets the job done!
